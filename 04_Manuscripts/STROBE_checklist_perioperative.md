## Notes on Ecological Study Adaptations

This checklist is adapted from the standard STROBE v4 cross-sectional format. Several items are reinterpreted for the ecological study design:

- **Item 6 (Participants)**: Refers to ecological units (prefectures), not individual participants.
- **Item 10 (Study size)**: N = 47 is fixed by the administrative structure of Japan; post-hoc power analysis is reported as a sensitivity measure.
- **Item 13c (Flow diagram)**: Not applicable; all 47 prefectures included with no exclusions.
- **Item 16c (Absolute risk)**: Not applicable for ecological regression.
